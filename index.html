Biography

Seung Jun Baek is a full professor of Computer Science and Engineering at Korea University. Prof. Baek received Ph.D. and M.S. degrees at University of Texas at Austin, and B.S. degree at Seoul National University, all in Electrical and Computer Engineering. He was a Member Technical Staff of DSP Systems R&D center, Communication and Medical Systems Lab. of Texas Instruments. He next joined Korea University: as of 2018, is a professor at Computer Science and Engr. Dept., College of Informatics, Korea University.

His research interests include machine learning/deep learning, mobile computing, signal processing, data-driven optimization, Internet of Things (IoT), and information/communication networks. He leads the System Intelligence & Networking Group (SING) with various research projects on computer vision, autonomous driving system, vehicular network, mobile edge computing.
