Biography

Seung Jun Baek is a full professor of Computer Science and Engineering Dept. at Korea University. His research interests include machine learning, mobile computing, data-driven optimization, deep learning, Internet of Things (IoT), and information/communication networks. He leads the System Intelligence & Networking Group with various research projects on computer vision, autonomous driving system, vehicular network, mobile edge computing.
